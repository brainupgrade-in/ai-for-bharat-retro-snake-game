# AI for Bharat

## Overview

AI for Bharat is a third-party program organized by **Hack2skill** with sponsorship support from **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**. This is an independent developer program - not directly administered by AWS. AWS provides sponsorship support, technical expertise access, and educational resources.

The program helps developers in India transition from AI theory to practice through guided learning and a national hackathon.

## Program Structure

### Phase 1: Learning & Certification
- **Structured workshops** - Deep-dive sessions on cloud fundamentals, best practices, and AI use cases in India
- **Hands-on labs** - Led by industry experts; participants build functional applications with real-time feedback
- **Weekly challenges** - Practice and apply new skills with rewards
- **AWS services covered**: Amazon Bedrock, Amazon Q, and Kiro
- **Certification requirement**: Complete workshop labs and publish a technical blog on AWS Builder Center

### Phase 2: National Hackathon
Participants apply their skills to solve real-world problems for India through a competitive hackathon.

## Two Tracks

| Track | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| **Student Track** | Innovative ideas, community-level solutions, demonstration of technical skills |
| **Professional/Startup Track** | Enterprise-grade applications, scalable models, market-ready solutions for critical societal problems |

## Eligibility

- Students (undergraduate/postgraduate)
- Full-time or part-time working professionals
- Startups building GenAI-powered features
- Independent developers/freelancers
